Pick trainer number
Most trainers use numbers 0-3. RIVAL_Artie uses 1-4 as the normal set, with Artie 0 as the common boss (shown together with Claude 4). EGADMIN_Claude uses 0-3 normally, with Claude 4 as the common boss. EGANOMALY_Christina shows both the first battle (0-3) and the second battle (+4) for the selected number.
